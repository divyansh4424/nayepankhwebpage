About us

Think global, act local.

NayePankh Foundation is a non-governmental organisation with one strong desire — to help society and make it a better place for everyone. Service to mankind is service to god. Let’s revolutionise society together.

We’re one of the biggest student-led NGOs in India, with operations across Kanpur, Ghaziabad and other cities — proudly run by young people, many still in school and college.

What we do

Four needs. One promise — dignity first.

We keep our work simple and direct, so help reaches people without losing its way.

Warm meals

No one should sleep hungry. We distribute food to families — and to the stray animals on their streets too.

Period dignity

Free sanitary napkins and honest menstrual-hygiene awareness, so no girl feels ashamed of something natural.

Clothing drives

Warm, clean clothes for poor families through the cold months and the rest of the year.

Education

Learning material and mentoring so children stay in school and build a better future.

How it started

From a handful of highschoolers to a movement.

“Badalte Bharat ki Nayi Tasveer” — the new picture of a changing India.

2020

The spark

As the world fought COVID-19, a group of highschoolers felt an urge to help with whatever they had. It was tough — but they didn’t lose hope.

28 March 2021

Boots on the ground

NayePankh officially landed to serve, driven by a simple belief: the real hope of a nation lies in the hands of its youth.

Later 2021

New wings

The mission widened beyond relief — food, sanitary napkins, clothes and education — and the name NayePankh was born: giving wings to uplift.

2021–22

Recognised & certified

Registered with the UP Government and certified 12A & 80G, so every donation earns 50% income-tax relief.

Today

2 lakh+ lives, and counting

One of India’s largest student-led NGOs — still hustling, because the goal isn’t complete yet.

Dear friends, NayePankh began with a simple vision — to uplift marginalised communities and give them access to education, healthcare and the basics so many of us take for granted.
